ADVENTURE

Adventure Time

If you're looking for adventure during your stay at our resort, you're in luck! We offer a variety of exciting activities for all ages. To get started, head over to our adventure desk where our knowledgeable staff can assist you in selecting the perfect adventure experience. We recommend booking in advance to ensure availability. For those looking for a thrilling outdoor adventure, our resort offers hiking, mountain biking, zip-lining, and rock climbing excursions. If you prefer water activities, we have kayaking, paddleboarding, and snorkeling available. Safety is our top priority, so all adventures are led by experienced guides and include all necessary equipment. Adventure timing varies based on the activity and the season, so be sure to check with our staff for the most up-to-date information. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced adventurer, we have something for everyone at our resort. Get ready for an unforgettable adventure!

BIKING

If you're planning to go biking during your stay at our resort, we have some helpful tips to ensure you have a safe and enjoyable experience. Firstly, make sure to wear appropriate clothing and footwear, including a helmet. We also recommend bringing sunscreen, a water bottle, and a small backpack to carry any essentials. Before hitting the trails, stop by our bike rental shop to select the perfect bike for your skill level and preferences. Our staff can provide maps and recommendations for the best biking routes in the area. When out on the trails, be aware of your surroundings and any potential hazards, such as rocks or steep drops. Stay on designated trails and respect any signage or trail closures. If you're a beginner, start with easier trails and work your way up to more challenging ones as your skills improve. Always bike within your limits and don't push yourself beyond what you're comfortable with. If you have any questions or concerns during your biking adventure, don't hesitate to contact our resort staff for assistance. Biking is a fantastic way to explore the beautiful surroundings of our resort, and we want you to have a safe and memorable experience.

PARA GLIDING

If you're looking for an exhilarating adventure during your stay at our resort, why not try paragliding? Before taking to the skies, there are a few important things to keep in mind. Firstly, all paragliding flights are weather dependent, so it's important to check with our staff for the latest weather conditions and to book your flight in advance. We offer tandem paragliding flights with experienced pilots, so no previous experience is necessary. You'll be given a full safety briefing and instruction on takeoff and landing procedures before your flight. Wear comfortable clothing and closed-toe shoes, and bring a jacket as it can be cooler at higher altitudes. Don't forget to bring a camera to capture the stunning views from above! During your flight, sit back and enjoy the experience, and feel free to ask your pilot any questions you may have. Paragliding is an exciting way to see the beautiful scenery around our resort, but safety is our top priority. All of our equipment is regularly inspected and maintained, and our pilots are trained and experienced. If you have any concerns or questions, don't hesitate to ask our staff. Get ready for an unforgettable paragliding adventure!

night safari

If you're looking for a unique and unforgettable experience during your stay at our resort, consider joining us on a night safari. Led by experienced guides, our night safari allows you to discover the nocturnal wildlife in the area, from elusive predators to fascinating insects. We use special lighting and vehicles that allow for a comfortable and safe experience while minimizing disturbance to the animals. The night safari typically lasts 2-3 hours and is available for an additional charge. We recommend booking in advance to secure your spot, as space is limited. The cost of the night safari is $75 per person, and this includes all necessary equipment, transportation, and the services of our knowledgeable guides. Children under the age of 12 are half-price. Dress comfortably and bring a camera to capture the unique sights you'll see on the night safari. Safety is our top priority, so please follow the instructions of our guides at all times. Get ready for an unforgettable adventure under the stars on our night safari!
